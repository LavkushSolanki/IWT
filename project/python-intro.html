Exam Instructions

Welcome to the test. This exam is designed to assess your understanding and knowledge of the subject, covering essential concepts, practical applications, and advanced topics. Please take a moment to carefully read the instructions and guidelines provided before starting. Ensure a focused and distraction-free environment to perform your best. Good luck!

Guidelines

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection before starting the quiz.
  • Once you begin the test, the timer will start, and pausing is not allowed.
  • Do not refresh or close the browser tab while the test is ongoing.
  • Each question carries equal marks, and there is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
  • Submit your answers before the timer runs out to avoid automatic submission.

Important Notes

  • The quiz duration is 15 minutes.
  • You will have 20 questions to attempt.
  • All questions are mandatory.

Technical Requirements

  • Compatible browsers: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Safari (latest versions).
  • Ensure your device is charged or connected to a power source.
  • Disable any browser extensions that might interfere with the quiz interface.

Tips for Success

  • Read each question carefully before answering.
  • Manage your time efficiently. Spend around 30-45 seconds on each question.
  • If you are unsure about a question, make an educated guess rather than skipping it.
  • Stay focused and avoid distractions during the test.

Code of Conduct

  • By starting the test, you agree to abide by the rules and maintain academic integrity.
  • Cheating, sharing answers, or using unauthorized resources is strictly prohibited.
  • Violations of these rules may result in disqualification or further action.
